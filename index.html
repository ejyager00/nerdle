NERDLE

This is a recreation of the Nerdle game as seen on nerdlegame.com. My source code is available on Github.

Ready to play?

Rules

  • The solution will always be a valid equation.
  • The right side of the equation will not have any binary operations, and the left side must have at least one.
  • After a guess, characters in green are in the correct spot for the solution, characters in yellow are in the solution in a different spot, and characters in red are not in the solution.
